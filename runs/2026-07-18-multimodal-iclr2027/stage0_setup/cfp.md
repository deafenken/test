## Topics of interest (non-exhaustive, from official 2026 CFP)

- unsupervised, self-supervised, semi-supervised, and supervised representation learning
- reinforcement learning
- generative models
- causal reasoning
- optimization
- learning theory
- graph learning
- fairness, privacy
- applications: robotics, neuroscience, physical sciences
- general machine learning

Multimodal / vision-language work sits squarely inside "representation learning" + "generative models";
ICLR historically has a large multimodal presence (VLM pretraining, multimodal reasoning, unified
tokenization, hallucination, efficient multimodal architectures).

## Review process

- Double-blind: reviewers cannot see author names; authors cannot see reviewer names.
- OpenReview platform; public reviews and author responses after decision.
- Final decisions for 2026 cycle were announced late January — expect late Jan 2027 for our cycle.
